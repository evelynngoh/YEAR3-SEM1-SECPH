During the Enterprise System Design and Modeling course, valuable understanding of enterprise information systems, enterprise architecture, and business process integration was developed. The course provided exposure to enterprise resource planning (ERP), enterprise architecture frameworks such as Zachman and TOGAF, system integration, data governance, customer relationship management, and implementation strategies used in modern organizations. Through project-based learning and industry-related activities with PPG, practical experience was gained in analyzing real-world enterprise problems, gathering system requirements, designing enterprise architectures, and developing suitable enterprise solutions. In addition, the course strengthened teamwork, communication, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills through collaborative projects, presentations, and industry engagements. Hands-on activities using modelling tools and discussions on real enterprise scenarios also improved understanding of how enterprise systems support organizational operations and decision-making processes. Overall, this course successfully enhanced both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in enterprise system design, modelling, and digital transformation within organizations.
