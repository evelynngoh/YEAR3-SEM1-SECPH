The French Language course provided valuable exposure to basic French communication skills and cultural understanding through topics such as greetings, self-introduction, grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and writing. Throughout the course, knowledge of French sentence structure, verbs, adjectives, and conversational expressions was gradually developed through quizzes, listening tests, oral presentations, and classroom activities. The course also strengthened communication, listening, and presentation skills while improving confidence in using a new language in both spoken and written forms. In addition, learning about French culture and Francophonie increased appreciation for cultural diversity and global communication. Overall, this course successfully enhanced basic language proficiency, interpersonal skills, and adaptability in learning a foreign language.
