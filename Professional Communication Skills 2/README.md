Professional Communication Skills 2 course provided valuable exposure to professional communication techniques, particularly in proposal writing, pitching, negotiation, and formal group discussions. Throughout the course, skills in presenting ideas clearly, organizing arguments systematically, and delivering professional presentations were significantly improved through various speaking and writing activities. The proposal and pitching assessments strengthened confidence in expressing ideas persuasively while enhancing critical thinking, communication, and presentation abilities in professional settings. In addition, negotiation and role-play activities improved teamwork, interpersonal communication, and adaptability by encouraging active participation in discussions and collaborative problem-solving. The course also increased awareness of the importance of effective communication in academic and workplace environments. Overall, this course successfully enhanced professional speaking, writing, and presentation skills while improving confidence in formal communication and group interaction.
